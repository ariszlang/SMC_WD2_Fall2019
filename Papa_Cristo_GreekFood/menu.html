Mousaka

Greek Moussaka is a flavorsome main dish combining several components. Main ingredients are usually minced lamb or beef meat, eggplant, potato and b√©chamel sauce. Garlic, grilled cheese and olive oil are also added. This tasty meal will keep you going a long time.

Stuffed Bell Peppers

Gemista is the name relating to stuffed vegetables. Eggplants, tomatoes, bell peppers and zucchini are usually filled with rice, herbs and olive oil. Ground meat sometimes substitutes rice. Once stuffed, the dish bakes in the oven

Greek Meatballs

Keftedakia, or traditional meatballs, is a savory addition to your Greek diet. Made from ground pork, beef or sheep meat fried in olive oil, meatballs are very juicy. They are typically served as appetizers and snacks.
