Welcome to
Hollywood Food Pantry

Hollywood Food Pantry provides assistance in the form of distributing food to those who have difficulty purchasing enough. Many of the lower income residents of Hollywood area live with food insecurity. This is a helpful place for residents to reach out for assistance so that we can foster community

Helping Others

We have many helpers that volunteer precious hours of their time to better serve you. they do this by collecting food, organizing our supplies, distributing food and participating in community outreach events.

Community

Hunger hurts our family members, neighbors and friends. We can help. Make an active and hands-on contribution to end hunger in Hollywood by hosting a Food or Fund Drive to benefit residents in Hollywood. you can help make a difference in our community you can connect with us for a food dive
